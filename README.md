# Bright_mind-_Coffee_Shop
The overview of the coffe shop performance, this analysis examines six months of historical sales data (1 January 2023 – 30 June 2023) from the Bright Coffee Shop chain across three locations: Lower Manhattan, Hell’s Kitchen, and Astoria.
The goal is to provide clear, actionable insights into daily operations, product performance, and revenue drivers so the CEO can make informed decisions to increase profitability and improve business performance.

Canva,Visual planning & presentation design
SQL ,Data analysis & aggregation
Excel,Final data tables & calculations
